Experience Lollapalooza through Lollagram

Music. Photography. Chicago.

With Lollapalooza upon us, the GolinHarris team brings you (yet again) Lollagram! Lollapalooza is one of the world’s largest music festivals and it takes place just down the street from where Al Golin started our company nearly 60 years ago.

Lollagram is a labor of love that’s designed to help you keep track of the Lollapalooza experience, through the eyes (or, more accurately, snapshots) of music lovers on the ground at Grant Park. Lollagram takes advantage of the Instagram API and aggregates the moments at Lollapalooza, creating a visual record of the bands, food, people, and city that make up this great musical tradition. With 45 million photos being uploaded to Instagram each day, we're excited to present snapshots from Lollapalooza 2013.

Lollagram will actively 'call' photos from the Instagram service and waits for a response, searching for photos and posts which include the word Lollapalooza. If you're attending Lollapalooza this year and will be sharing photos via Instagram, we hope you’ll include #Lollapalooza so your photos will be featured on Lollagram!

If you like Lollagram, please help us spread the word through the sharing tools on the site homepage. Enjoy Lollapalooza 2013!

-Your Friends at GolinHarris
